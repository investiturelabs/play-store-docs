Delete Your Pockets Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the Pockets app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Pockets app on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Type DELETE to confirm
  6. Tap the confirmation button

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile information and email
  • All scheduled tasks and activities
  • Fixed events and calendar data
  • Completion history and streaks
  • All preferences and settings
